  • Mumbai Indians Clinch Second Women's Premier League Title with Eight-Run Victory Over Delhi Capitals

    In a thrilling final at Brabourne Stadium on March 15, 2025, Mumbai Indians (MI) secured their second Women's Premier League (WPL) title by defeating Delhi Capitals (DC) by eight runs. This victory marked MI's dominance in the tournament, having previously won the inaugural season in 2023.

  • IPL 2025 Kicks Off with Star-Studded Inaugural Ceremony

    The 2025 Indian Premier League (IPL) season officially begins on March 22, with a grand opening ceremony featuring Bollywood stars and live performances. This year’s tournament promises high-octane action and fierce competition. Chennai Super Kings (CSK), with their strong lineup and the leadership of MS Dhoni, are among the top contenders to claim the title. Dhoni’s strategic brilliance and experience give CSK a significant edge in their pursuit of a sixth IPL championship. Alongside CSK, Mumbai Indians (MI) are also strong favorites, with a deep squad and excellent form heading into the season.
