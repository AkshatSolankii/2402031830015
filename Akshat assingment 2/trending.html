  • MS Dhoni Lands in Chennai Ahead of IPL 2025: Fans Eager for His Return

  • India Triumphs Over New Zealand to Secure 2025 Champions Trophy

    In a thrilling final at Dubai International Cricket Stadium, India clinched the 2025 ICC Champions Trophy by defeating New Zealand by four wickets. Batting first, New Zealand posted a total of 251 runs, with significant contributions from Michael Bracewell (53 not out) and Daryl Mitchell (63). India's bowlers, led by spinners Kuldeep Yadav and Varun Chakravarthy, played pivotal roles in restricting New Zealand's scoring. Chasing the target, Indian captain Rohit Sharma top-scored with a resilient 76 runs. Despite a late surge from New Zealand's spinners, India achieved the target with nine balls to spare, finishing the tournament unbeaten.
